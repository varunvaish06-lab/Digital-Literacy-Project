‘Stay Safe Online’ Checklist
✅ Prevention Tips
-Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
-Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
-Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails
-Always verify website URLs before entering details
-Keep your devices and apps updated
-Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
-Log out from shared devices after use
-Regularly monitor your bank statements
💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
-Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
-Double-check receiver details before making payments
-Avoid scanning unknown QR codes
-Use official banking apps only
🚨 Report Cybercrime
-National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-Helpline Number: 1930
